The First Coffee

Emma wakes up early on a quiet morning. The streets are calm, and the air feels fresh. Today is special for her because she plans to visit a café for the first time.

She walks slowly and finds a small coffee shop on the corner. The door opens with a soft sound, and the smell of coffee fills the air. It feels warm and welcoming inside.

Emma looks at the menu and feels a little nervous. She has never ordered coffee before. The barista smiles and asks, “What would you like?” Emma takes a deep breath and says, “A latte, please.”

She sits by the window and waits. Soon, her drink arrives in a simple white cup. She takes her first sip. The taste is smooth and warm. She smiles.

In that moment, Emma feels happy. It is not just coffee. It is a new experience, a small step into something new. She looks outside and enjoys the quiet morning with her first coffee.
