The Lost Key

Leo had always been curious about the old attic in his grandmother’s house. It was a quiet place, full of forgotten memories and dusty furniture. One afternoon, while cleaning, he noticed a small wooden box hidden behind a pile of books.

Inside the box, he found a strange golden key. It looked old, but it was shining as if it was new. There was also a small note that read, “Some doors are hidden for a reason.”

As he continued searching the attic, he discovered a locked door behind a curtain. He was sure it had not been there before. With a deep breath, he held the key and slowly walked toward the door.

When he placed the key into the lock, it fit perfectly. The door opened with a soft click. Inside, he found a secret room filled with old maps, letters, and photographs.

Leo realized that the key was not just an object—it was a beginning. A mystery that he was now part of.
